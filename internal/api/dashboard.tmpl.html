Add smolanalytics product analytics to this app. Load the script {{.Base}}/sdk.js and call smolanalytics.init("{{if .WriteKey}}{{.WriteKey}}{{else}}YOUR_WRITE_KEY{{end}}", {{"{"}} host: "{{.Base}}" {{"}"}}). Then call smolanalytics.track(event, props) at the key moments — "signup" on account creation, "activate" at the core aha moment, "checkout" on payment — and smolanalytics.identify(userId) on login. Put the calls in the right places in the codebase.
